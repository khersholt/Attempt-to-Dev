# Azure Email Orchestrator - Finding an Email Template in AEO

All previously built email templates can be located in the Azure Email Orchestrator [Template Gallery](https://emails.azure.net/gallery/).

![Gallery](./media/aeo-template-gallery/aeo-template-gallery/gallery-overview.png)

## Searching for an Email Template

1. To find your email template, enter some keywords into the search bar:  

	![Search](./media/aeo-template-gallery/aeo-template-gallery/gallery-search.png)

2. Click on "More Information" at the bottom for more details about this template.
3. Click on "Preview in a new Window" to view the email preview link.

  ![Preview](./media/aeo-template-gallery/aeo-template-gallery/template-preview.png)

4. If your email template requires an update, click on the blue "Feedback" tab on the right side of the browser window to submit a [UserSnap](https://acom.azure.net/documentation/aeo-feedback-usersnap/).

  ![UserSnap](./media/aeo-template-gallery/aeo-template-gallery/usersnap-feedback.png)
  

	> [AZURE.WARNING] If you do not receive an email from **UserSnap** with a link to your work item within one-to-five minutes, there may be an issue with the **UserSnap** integration. If this is the case, please contact the **ACOM Web Team** for further assistance.
